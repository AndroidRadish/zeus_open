## Decision Rationale

Wave 1 was designed as a parallel execution proof-of-concept for v2. The three tasks (store.py, agent_bus.py, workflow_graph.py) have zero cross-dependencies, making them ideal candidates for concurrent Agent dispatch. This validates both the technical feasibility of parallel infrastructure development and the project management hypothesis behind the multi-agent v2 redesign.
